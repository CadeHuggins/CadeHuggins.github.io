Welcome to Stickman Parkour

Overview

Stickman Parkour is an exciting action game where you control a stickman and help him navigate challenging parkour obstacles. Try to make it through each level by jumping, climbing, and avoiding obstacles!

How to Play

Follow the steps below to play the game:

  1. Click on the game to start playing.
  2. Use the arrow keys to move your character.
  3. Press the up arrow to jump over obstacles.
  4. Try to get as far as you can without falling or hitting obstacles!
